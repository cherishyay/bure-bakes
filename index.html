Our Story

At our bakery, we believe the simplest ingredients create the most unforgettable flavors. What started as a passion for baking fresh, comforting bread has grown into a space where tradition meets trend — and every bite feels special.

Bure Bakes was founded as a collaborative project for our Multimedia class by Cherish, Brandon, Kenji, Juan, and Michelle. We shared a vision of creating a digital space that feels as warm and inviting as a real kitchen, blending our love for aesthetics with the art of baking.

Whether you’re stopping by for your morning bread, an afternoon treat, or a sweet gift for someone special, we bake everything with care, quality ingredients, and a whole lot of heart.

Frequently Asked Questions

[ Ordering and Customization ]

Do I need to pre-order my bread?

While we keep our shelves stocked daily, our signature sourdough and specialty loaves tend to fly out the door by noon. If you have your heart set on a specific bake, we recommend placing an order through our site 24 hours in advance.

Do you offer custom cakes or large event orders?

We love being part of your celebrations! We offer custom cakes and catering platters for events. Please reach out via our [Contact Form] at least 7 days prior to your event so we can perfect the details.

[ Ingredients and Dietary Needs ]

Do you have gluten-free or vegan options?

We sure do! We bake a selection of vegan cookies and gluten-friendly brownies daily. However, please note that we are a traditional flour-based bakery; while we do our best to prevent cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee a 100% flour-free environment for those with severe allergies.

Where do you source your ingredients?

Quality is everything to us. We use organic, stone-ground flour from local mills and seasonal fruits from nearby farms.

[ Shipping & Pickup ]

Do you ship your treats nationwide?

Currently, we focus on serving our local community through in-store pickup and local delivery in Gading Serpong. Bread is best when it's fresh!

How should I store my bread to keep it fresh?

Because we don’t use artificial preservatives, our bread is best enjoyed within 2–3 days. Store it in a cool, dry place or slice and freeze it for later!
