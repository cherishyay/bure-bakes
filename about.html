Our Story

At our bakery, we believe the simplest ingredients create the most unforgettable flavors. What started as a passion for baking fresh, comforting bread has grown into a space where tradition meets trend — and every bite feels special.

Bure Bakes was founded as a collaborative project for our Multimedia class by members consisting of:

  • Brandon Andrew Valerian,
  • Cherish Valini Masyap,
  • Juan Gunawan,
  • Kenji Viriya Otang, and
  • Michelle Vandana Putri Hermawan
We shared a vision of creating a digital space that feels as warm and inviting as a real kitchen, blending our love for aesthetics with the art of baking.

Whether you’re stopping by for your morning bread, an afternoon treat, or a sweet gift for someone special, we bake everything with care, quality ingredients, and a whole lot of heart.

Freshly baked. Boldly flavored. Made to share!
